Featured Slack Channels

We've got over 100 channels, sounds crazy right? It works though. Here are a few featured channels you'll get dropped in to right away:

  • #announcements - this is our default channel that you can't leave. It's usually quiet in here, but anyone is welcome to post an announcement regarding meetups, app/site launches, life announcements, and so on.
  • #general - general chit-chat goes on here, and you can leave it if it gets too noisy.
  • #help - stuck on a problem? Ask here. You may get redirected to a more specific #help channel (like #help-css), just to keep things tidy.
  • #intros - say hello!
  • #topic-gigs - drop open positions and "looking for work" posts here.
  • #topic-random - anything you want (within reason)
  • #topic-community - the place to discuss Denver Devs features, updates, ideas, and general community.

Want to know more? Check out the Slack Guide we've made specifically for Denver Devs

Code of Conduct

Denver Devs is dedicated to providing a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of gender identity or expression, sexual orientation, disability, physical appearance, body size, race, religion or non-religion. We do not tolerate harassment of participants in any form. Harassment includes offensive verbal comments related to gender, sexual orientation, disability, physical appearance, body size, race, religion, sexual images, deliberate intimidation, stalking, sustained disruption of discussions or other events, and unwelcome sexual attention. If a participant engages in behavior that violates this code of conduct, the organizers may take any action they deem appropriate, including warning the offender or expulsion from the group.
